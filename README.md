# 📊 Sales Profitability Optimization

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project analyzes sales data to identify profitability issues and provide business recommendations using Excel, SQL, and Power BI.

---

## 🧠 Business Problem
The company is generating good sales but not maximizing profit due to:
- High discounts
- Loss-making products
- Regional inefficiencies

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Identify profit and loss areas
- Analyze impact of discount on profit
- Improve overall profit margin

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Excel (Data Cleaning)
- SQL (Data Analysis)
- Power BI (Dashboard)

## ⚙️ SQL Analysis (Actual Work Done)

### 1. Overall Company Performance
- Calculated total sales, total profit, and profit margin
- Found ~12% profit margin

### 2. Region Wise Analysis
- Compared sales and profit across regions
- Identified underperforming regions

### 3. Loss Making Products
- Found products with negative profit
- These products need review or removal

### 4. Discount Impact Analysis
- Analyzed how discount affects profit
- Found that higher discounts lead to losses

### 5. Category Analysis
- Compared performance by category and region
- Measured profit margin for each category

### 6. Sub-Category Loss Analysis
- Identified sub-categories generating losses
- Focus areas for improvement

### 7. Segment Analysis
- Analyzed customer segments
- Found some segments less profitable

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Company has ~12% profit margin
- High discounts reduce profitability
- Some products and sub-categories are loss-making
- Certain regions and segments underperform

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Reduce high discount strategies
- Remove or improve loss-making products
- Focus on profitable regions and segments
- Optimize pricing strategy
